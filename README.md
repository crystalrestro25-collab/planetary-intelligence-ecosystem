# Planetary Intelligence Ecosystem

The Planetary Intelligence Ecosystem is a global initiative developed under **ERAYA Intelligence System** to build integrated intelligence frameworks across three fundamental domains:

• Human Health  
• Earth Systems  
• Planetary Protection  

The ecosystem recognizes that **human health, environmental balance and planetary sustainability are deeply interconnected** and require integrated intelligence systems to address global challenges.

---

## Ecosystem Domains

### ERAYA – Human Health Intelligence

ERAYA is a Human Health Intelligence Platform focused on personalized, preventive and integrative healthcare systems.

The platform explores the integration of traditional knowledge systems with modern data science and artificial intelligence to generate meaningful health intelligence.

Key focus areas:

- Personalized health intelligence  
- Preventive healthcare systems  
- Integrative wellness intelligence  
- Health data analytics  

Repository:  
https://github.com/eraya-intelligence-system/ERAYA

### Bhumisetu – Land & Environmental Intelligence

Bhumisetu focuses on land systems, environmental monitoring and ecological intelligence to support sustainable development and responsible land governance.

Key focus areas:

- Land intelligence systems  
- Environmental monitoring  
- Ecological balance analytics  
- Sustainable infrastructure planning  

Repository:  
https://github.com/eraya-intelligence-system/Bhumisetu

---

### BhumiRaksha – Planetary Protection Intelligence

BhumiRaksha focuses on planetary protection through intelligent waste management, circular resource systems and climate resilience frameworks.

Key focus areas:

- Waste intelligence systems  
- Circular resource economy  
- Waste-to-energy frameworks  
- Climate impact analytics
