# Ecosystem Overview

The Planetary Intelligence Ecosystem is an integrated framework designed to connect three core domains:

Human Health  
Environmental Systems  
Planetary Protection  

These domains are represented through three platforms developed under ERAYA Intelligence System:

ERAYA – Human Health Intelligence  
Bhumisetu – Land & Environmental Intelligence  
BhumiRaksha – Planetary Protection Intelligence  

The ecosystem aims to create a unified intelligence framework capable of addressing complex global challenges through interdisciplinary systems thinking.
