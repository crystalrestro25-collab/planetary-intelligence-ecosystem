
# Planetary Intelligence Ecosystem

## Introduction

The ERAYA Intelligence Ecosystem is a multidisciplinary initiative focused on developing integrated intelligence systems across three fundamental domains:

Human Health  
Earth Systems  
Planetary Protection  

The ecosystem aims to connect health intelligence, environmental intelligence and planetary sustainability frameworks to address complex global challenges.

---

## Ecosystem Vision

To build an integrated intelligence ecosystem that enhances human wellbeing while protecting environmental and planetary systems.

The ecosystem recognizes that human health, environmental balance and planetary sustainability are deeply interconnected.

---

## Core Platforms

### ERAYA – Human Health Intelligence

ERAYA focuses on personalized and preventive health intelligence systems.  
The platform explores the integration of traditional knowledge systems with modern data science and artificial intelligence to enable intelligent wellness insights.

Key focus areas include:

• Personalized health intelligence  
• Preventive healthcare systems  
• Integrative wellness intelligence  
• Health data analytics  

---

### Bhumisetu – Land & Environmental Intelligence

Bhumisetu develops intelligence systems for understanding land ecosystems, environmental dynamics and sustainable development.

Key focus areas include:

• Land intelligence systems  
• Environmental monitoring  
• Ecological balance analytics  
• Sustainable infrastructure planning  

---

### BhumiRaksha – Planetary Protection Intelligence

BhumiRaksha focuses on planetary protection systems addressing waste management, circular resource systems and climate resilience.

Key focus areas include:

• Waste intelligence systems  
• Circular resource economy  
• Waste-to-energy frameworks  
• Climate impact analytics
